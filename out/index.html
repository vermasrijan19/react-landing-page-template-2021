ACM Semicode
Is back

SemiCode is a tag team coding contest that takes place in Mumbai and is organized by NMIMS Mumbai's technical body, ACM MPSTME in association with other colleges such as SNDT, MHSSCE, and FRCRCE. This competition brings together some of the brightest minds in the field of competitive coding from across Mumbai. The competition is designed to test the participants' problem-solving skills, time management, and ability to coordinate as a team.

About the competition

Round 1

This will be an online round, where participants would have to crack a set of questions assigned to your team within a duration of 2 hours on the day of the competition. Starts on 19th March '23!

Round 2

Round 2 will commence offline, with intermediate level of questions. Show your team's individual coding excellence and prove your mettle! Offline from 10 AM, 25 March 2023 at MPSTME, Mumbai.

Round 3

Beat round 2? Buck up and compete against the best coding tag-teams of Mumbai, and claim your spot on the leaderboard! Offline from 2 PM, 25 March 2023 at MPSTME, Mumbai.
